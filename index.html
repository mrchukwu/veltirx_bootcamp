Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any programming experience to join?

No prior programming experience is required! Our bootcamp is designed for complete beginners. We start with the very basics and gradually build up to more advanced concepts. We also welcome intermediate developers who want to strengthen their JavaScript fundamentals.

What equipment do I need for the bootcamp?

You'll need a computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) with a reliable internet connection. We'll guide you through setting up all necessary software including a code editor, Git, and Node.js during the first week.

How much time should I dedicate to the bootcamp?

We recommend dedicating 15-20 hours per week. This includes 8 hours of live instruction, 4-6 hours of homework, and 3-6 hours of project work. The schedule is flexible to accommodate working professionals.

What kind of job support do you provide?

Our career support includes resume review, portfolio development guidance, mock interviews, salary negotiation tips, and access to our hiring partner network. We also provide ongoing mentorship for 6 months after graduation.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the bootcamp within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of all projects and assessments, you'll receive a certificate of completion that's recognized by our industry partners and can be added to your LinkedIn profile.
